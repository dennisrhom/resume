Miniature UAV Design: "Rama" Aircraft

SolidWorksWind TunnelAerodynamicsCFD

Overview

This project involved conceptualizing and designing a detailed CAD model of the fictional "Dragonfly" aircraft from Arthur C. Clarke's novel "Rendezvous with Rama". The design was based on 3 key descriptions from the novel, aiming for theoretical compatibility with subsonic wind tunnel testing.

Design & Testing

  • Conceptualized a detailed CAD model of the fictional aircraft using SolidWorks, ensuring it adhered to the novel's descriptions.
  • Engineered specific attachments for the Solidworks CAD model to ensure 100% compatibility with subsonic wind tunnel testing rigs.
  • Conducted 9+ wind tunnel tests, analyzing both qualitative flow visualizations (e.g., smoke wands) and quantitative data across 3 different flow environments/Reynolds numbers.
  • The analysis focused on understanding the aerodynamic behavior and potential performance limitations of the craft in Earth-like conditions.
