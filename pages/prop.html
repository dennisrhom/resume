Propeller-Thrust Optimization

LabVIEW Data Acquisition Aerodynamics

Overview

Conducted in Austin, TX, this project involved engineering high-precision experiments to analyze the efficiency of various propeller twist configurations using advanced sensors and data processing.

Technical Approach

  • Sensor Integration: Utilized strain gages and photodiode sensors to capture high-frequency performance data.
  • Data Processing: Collected raw voltage data and applied calculated calibration constants to derive real-world thrust and RPM values.
  • Testing Suite: Conducted 12+ trials using LabVIEW to compare efficiency across 3 propellers with pitch configurations ranging from 5° to 6.5°.
